Who Are We?

Welcome to Four4CMe, your trusted source for premium, high-quality hair products with a touch of culture and heritage. We are a proudly Black-owned and operated business, founded by Ntando Nokilana, a passionate entrepreneur who recognized the need for hair care products that celebrate the beauty and diversity of Black hair.

Our Story

Four4CMe was born out of a deep appreciation for the rich tapestry of Black hair. Our journey began when Ntando decided to channel their passion for natural hair into creating a brand that would cater to the unique needs and desires of the Black community. With years of experience in the beauty industry and a commitment to promoting self-confidence, Ntando set out on a mission to empower individuals to embrace their natural beauty.

Our Mission

At Four4CMe, our mission is to provide exceptional hair care products that not only nourish and enhance the natural beauty of Black hair but also celebrate the cultural significance and diversity of our community. We are dedicated to delivering products that help you feel confident, stylish, and proud of your unique heritage.

What Sets Us Apart?

Quality: We believe in using only the finest ingredients and adhering to the highest industry standards to create products that promote healthy hair and stunning results.

Culture-Centric: Our products are designed with an understanding of the diversity of Black hair and the cultural importance it holds. We take pride in celebrating the history and beauty of our community through our offerings.

Empowerment: Four4CMe is more than just a hair company; it's a movement. We are committed to empowering individuals to embrace their natural beauty and feel confident in their own skin.

Community Engagement: We actively engage with and support the Black community by participating in local events, supporting initiatives, and promoting self-esteem and self-expression.

Our Products

Our product range includes a variety of hair care essentials, from nourishing shampoos and conditioners to styling products that cater to various textures and needs. Whether you have tightly coiled curls, loose waves, or something in between, our products are formulated to make your hair look and feel its best.
